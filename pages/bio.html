BIO

Jules Daud, hailing from the vibrant city of Buenos Aires, Argentina, embarked on a musical journey that spanned continents and cultures. His story began with a move to Minneapolis, a city known for its rich musical tapestry, where he took his first steps in honing his craft. From there, Jules found himself drawn to the magnetic energy of Brooklyn, New York City, where he decided to set down roots and give life to his musical vision. A versatile artist, Jules Daud wears many hats in the world of music. He's not just a singer; he's a songwriter, composer, and a master of the guitar. His sound is a testament to the fusion of influences that have shaped his life. Drawing inspiration from the heartland of American Blues and Rock, he infuses his music with the soulful sounds and rhythms of his South American roots. In the bustling streets of New York City, Jules found his muse. His lyrics tell stories of urban reality, the vibrant nightlife, and the bohemian lifestyle that pulses through the city's veins. But his songs also speak to deeper themes, touching on the immigrant experience and the struggles of the working class. One remarkable aspect of Jules' journey is his ability to connect with audiences. Singing predominantly in Spanish, he created a unique space in the heart of Manhattan and Brooklyn where people from all walks of life came together to experience the fusion of cultures and sounds. He has found a devoted fan base who share in the passion of this musical adventure, making his performances a memorable and soulful experience in the heart of the city. Jules Daud is more than just an artist; he's a bridge between worlds, bringing a piece of South America to the streets of New York, and in doing so, creating a musical tapestry that resonates with the soul of the city.
