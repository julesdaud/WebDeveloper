MUSIC

Since 2014, Jules Daud's music career has taken him on a remarkable journey through the United States. In 2015 and 2016, he recorded an album with The LoneSomeKid at the iconic Dial Back Sound studios in Water Valley, Mississippi, followed by collaborations with Sudaka at Minnehaha Recording Company Minneapolis, Minnesota. His pivotal chapter began in Brooklyn, NYC, where he launched his solo project, fusing South American roots with American Blues and Rock. In November 2021, he recorded an album at BC Studio, guided by legendary producer Martin Bisi (known for his work with Iggy Pop, Sonic Youth, and more). The album's final touch was added during mixing at Estudio del Parral in Buenos Aires. Jules Daud's journey represents the convergence of diverse influences and unwavering commitment to artistic evolution, reflecting the vibrant energy of NYC's music scene. In June 2021, he created 5 new songs at Wild Sound Recording Studios in Minneapolis, working with producer Matt Holmes. These tracks were mixed by Pablo Martin at the Harlem Punk in New York City in 2022 and released as an EP in 2023. Jules Daud's music seamlessly blends diverse influences and captures the essence of vibrant New York City.
